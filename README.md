## Featured Nations

### Поліщуки (Polishchuks) - PSH
The people of the Polissian marshlands - unique culture of forest and swamp dwellers in the Pripyat basin.
- **Capital**: Pinsk (278)
- **Culture**: Ruthenian
- **Religion**: Orthodox
- **Key Features**: Marshland defense, forest economy, local autonomy
- **Formation**: Available to Ruthenian/Belarusian nations controlling Pinsk, Turov, and Mazyr
