# * UZH_laws: tax + development cost reduction captures Magdeburg-inspired
#   municipal reforms; feeds economic branches of mission tree.
# * UZH_mountain_faith: universal tolerance fosters identity-branch missions
#   (Ruthenian/Rusyn/Uhro-Rusyn) and supports Union Synod reform.
# * UZH_free_trade: trade power + goods size depict Huszt as salt-caravan hub
#   linking Tokaj wine trade and Solotvyno salt.
# * UZH_roads: extra diplo upkeep + improve relations accelerate Danubian
#   diplomacy (Pressburg Compact, Carpathian Federation missions).

#   mountain guerrilla mobility motifs inspiring Smereka idea.
